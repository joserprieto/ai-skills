# Panel Template: Document Review

Use this template when evaluating reports, proposals, RFCs, whitepapers, or any written deliverable.

## Suggested areas and evaluators

### Area 1: Content accuracy and completeness (3-5 evaluators)

| ID    | Specialisation                 | Focus                                                          |
| ----- | ------------------------------ | -------------------------------------------------------------- |
| CON-1 | Subject matter accuracy        | Are the facts, data, and claims correct?                       |
| CON-2 | Completeness and coverage      | Does it cover everything it should?                            |
| CON-3 | Source quality and attribution | Are sources cited, credible, and properly attributed?          |
| CON-4 | Logical coherence              | Does the argument flow? Are conclusions supported by evidence? |
| CON-5 | Timeliness and relevance       | Is the information current and relevant to its purpose?        |

### Area 2: Writing quality and professionalism (3-5 evaluators)

| ID    | Specialisation              | Focus                                               |
| ----- | --------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------- |
| WRI-1 | Structure and organisation  | Is the document well-organised with clear sections? |
| WRI-2 | Clarity and readability     | Can the target audience understand it?              |
| WRI-3 | Executive summary quality   | Does the summary accurately represent the content?  |
| WRI-4 | Actionability               | Are recommendations concrete and implementable?     |
| WRI-5 | Formatting and presentation | Tables, figures, consistent style?                  |

### Area 3: Fitness for purpose (2-4 evaluators)

| ID    | Specialisation               | Focus                                                  |
| ----- | ---------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------ |
| FIT-1 | Audience alignment           | Is the document appropriate for its intended audience? |
| FIT-2 | Compliance with standards    | Does it meet relevant standards or templates?          |
| FIT-3 | Cost/value assessment        | Was the effort proportionate to the value delivered?   |
| FIT-4 | Comparison with alternatives | How does it compare to what was available before?      |

## Suggested minimum panel

For a quick review: CON-1, CON-2, WRI-1, WRI-2, FIT-1 (5 evaluators, 1 batch)

For a thorough review: All evaluators above (12-14 evaluators, 3 batches)
